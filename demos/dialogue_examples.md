# Dialogue Examples

This demo set illustrates how different synthetic profiles can react differently to the same service problem.

## Analytical profile
- Focuses on clarification, status, and next steps.
- Tends to ask for confirmation before escalation.

Example:
> Quero confirmar o que foi corrigido e o que ainda esta pendente antes de seguir.

## Reactive social profile
- Gives more weight to treatment quality and relational friction.
- Escalates faster when the issue stays unresolved.

Example:
> Isso ja esta me desgastando mais do que deveria, e o atendimento piorou a situacao.

## Defensive instability-sensitive profile
- Prioritizes immediate action and concrete resolution.
- Uses shorter and firmer language under pressure.

Example:
> Isso passou do limite aceitavel. Quero o proximo passo objetivo e um prazo claro.
